🔑 TARGET VOCABULARY
supply chain
All steps from raw material to the final customer.
vendor
A company that supplies you with goods or materials.
raw materials
Basic ingredients before any manufacturing.
inventory
Stock of goods stored waiting to be sold.
JIT
Just-In-Time: receive supplies only when needed.
distribution centre
Warehouse that sends goods out to shops.
logistics provider
Company paid to transport and deliver goods.
reverse logistics
Handling returned or unsold goods after sale.
validate
To officially check and confirm supplier quality.
